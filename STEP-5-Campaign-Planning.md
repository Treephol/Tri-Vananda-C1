# STEP 5: Campaign Planning & Key Messages

Two parallel Meta Ads campaigns. Same funnel (Ad → LP → WhatsApp → Visit). Same budget split (THB 50K each / 30 days). Opposite buyer psychologies, opposite creative temperatures.

---

## C1 — Green · Slow Burn

- **Persona:** Business Tycoon with Family
- **Audience:** Age 35+ · HK, Singapore, Bangkok, Shanghai, Sydney, Melbourne
- **Archetype:** Connoisseur / Achiever — legacy and longevity
- **Stage of psychology:** Permission — *"Is this the right operating base for the next chapter of my family?"*
- **Single insight:** The wealthy aren't moving for tax. They're moving for environments that match the standard they hold internally.
- **Single idea:** Where Asia's wealth is moving — and why now
- **Single feeling:** The quiet relief of recognition — *this is the place I have been looking for*
- **Objective:** Traffic → LP Views (switch to Lead at 50+ events)
- **Targeting:** Wellness · Luxury Real Estate · Intl. Travellers · Family & Wealth · 1% LAL
- **Tone:** Editorial, family-led, high-context, never selling
- **Key proof points:** 232 acres · 85% forest, farm, water · Net zero · 15 min from airport · Interiors by AvroKO · 4 architecture firms · Michelin Green Star Jampa on-site · 600 rai of central Phuket
- **Hero lines:** *"Live in Balance with Nature"* · *"232 Acres of Forest, Farm, and Water"* · *"A Home for Every Generation"* · *"Built to Last Decades"*
- **Forecast:** 2,000–3,300 LP visits · 100–165 WA chats · 1–2 qualified visits
- **Success metric:** WA click rate >5% of LP visitors

---

## C2 — Gold · Fast Impulse

- **Persona:** Impulse Buyer + Thailand Partners
- **Audience:** Age 35–55 · Phuket, Phang Nga · Bangkok (Sukhumvit, Sathorn, Thonglor)
- **Archetype:** Achiever / Experientialist with a Curator's eye — they buy what they recognise, not what they're told to want
- **Stage of psychology:** Desire ↔ Recognition — the moment a property stops being a property and becomes the version of life they've been quietly designing in their head
- **Single insight:** They're not shopping for square metres. They're shopping for the version of themselves that already lives there — and they'll know it inside the first thirty seconds.
- **Single idea:** The taste you've been building was always pointing here
- **Single feeling:** The slow exhale of recognition — *this is what I meant*
- **Objective:** Leads → Highest Volume
- **Targeting:** Design & Architecture · Boutique Hotels · Wellness · Slow Food · Modern Art · Couples & Creative Founders · 2–3% LAL from CRM
- **Dayparting:** Thu 14:00 – Mon 23:00 — the long-weekend daydreaming window
- **Tone:** Sensory, lifestyle-led, atmospheric — the ad is a feeling, not an offer. Never explains. Never sells.
- **Key proof points:** Mornings in the forest before the city wakes · Michelin Green Star Jampa inside the gate · Wellness on a walk, not a calendar · A pool you actually swim in · Neighbours who recognise the same things you do
- **Hero lines:** *"The Place You Were Already Building in Your Head"* · *"Taste Recognises Taste"* · *"Where the Weekend Stops Being a Weekend"* · *"Your Life, Held Differently"*
- **Forecast:** 2,700–5,000 clicks · 220–450 WA chats · 1–3 qualified visits
- **Success metric:** Click-to-WA 8–12% · Visit-booking >0.5% of conversations

- **Context is brand** — psychographic, not demographic
- **Two engines, one world** — prestige (C1) + community (C2)

I — The philosophy
I — The philosophy
"Tri Vananda was born from a family's personal journey through illness and recovery — a belief that living well is not a luxury, but a way of life rooted in nature, community, and genuine human connection."
The meaning of the name
"Tri" represents brotherhood and multigenerational community. "Vana" is the Thai word for forest — reflecting biophilic design and connection to nature. "Nanda" means ultimate happiness — affirming a life of longevity and joy.
The vision
A multigenerational well-living community where like-minded people come to heal body and soul, embrace nature, and interact with community — learning how to become healthier and happier, together.

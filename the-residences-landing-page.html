The Residences

85% Forest, Farm, and Water.
The Land Was Here First. The Architecture Respects That.

Only 15% of 232 acres is built on. Nine lakes, regrown forest, and a working organic farm are not amenities — they are the foundation. Net zero-emission by design. Biophilic architecture where timber, stone, and glass sit inside the landscape, not on top of it. The food comes from the soil. The air is shaped by the canopy. Time here moves at the speed of nature.

Two to five bedroom private pool villas, built to hold their character over decades. Sustainability is not a feature — it is how everything was drawn, sourced, and placed. A home that gives back more than it takes, in a community that does the same.

Four Firms. One Standard.
Built to Last Decades.
